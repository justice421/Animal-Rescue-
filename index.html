Info

Below this will be some information and pictures of just a few endangered animals

tiger

Tigers are the biggest cat in their species. It's most recognizable for it's stripes. It's classified as a genus in it's species.

black-footed ferret

The black-footed ferret, also known as the American polecat or prairie dog hunter, is a species of mustelid native to central North America. It is listed as endangered by the IUCN, because of its very small and restricted populations.

pangolins

Pangolins have large, protective keratin scales covering their skin; they are the only known mammals with this feature. They live in hollow trees or burrows.
